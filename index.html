About

I am a PhD researcher at Nottingham Trent University exploring intercultural frameworks in textile and nature partnerships through indigenous wool practices in India. My work centres around sustainability, decolonisation, and pluriversal design.

Consultancy & Projects

  • Next Door Repairs – Research Lead for Innovate UK-funded project on mending and repair culture in Hackney.
  • Making for Change – Impact evaluation assistant, contributing to REF-quality analysis.
  • Centre for Sustainable Fashion – Contributor to Innovate UK report on fashion and sustainability.

Publications & Talks

  • "Desi Oon: Unstitched Textile Narratives of Rajasthan and Himachal Pradesh" – Forthcoming in Fashion’s Fibres as Planetary Flows
  • "Textile and Nature Partnerships" – Accepted for Prosperity Fashion Conference 2025
